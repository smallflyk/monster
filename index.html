Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game where you'll face endless hordes of monsters. Your mission is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating enemies and collecting power-ups to become stronger.

With each level up, you'll gain access to new abilities and upgrades, allowing you to create a unique build for your character. Will you focus on speed, attack power, or defense? The choice is yours in this fast-paced adventure!

How to Play

Controls

  • Use WASD or ↑↓←→ keys to move your character
  • Attacks are automatic when enemies are in range
  • Press Space to use special abilities (when available)
  • ESC key to pause the game

Tips & Tricks

  • Keep moving to avoid getting surrounded
  • Collect experience gems to level up faster
  • Balance your upgrades for maximum effectiveness
  • Some enemies drop health when defeated
